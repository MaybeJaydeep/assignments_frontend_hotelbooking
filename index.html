About Us

History of JustStay

Founded in 2016, JustStay has been a beacon of hospitality in the heart of Ahmedabad. What started as a vision to provide exceptional accommodation has grown into one of the city's most trusted and beloved hotels. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a space where guests feel at home while experiencing the rich culture and warmth of Gujarat.

Why Choose us?

At JustStay, we believe that every stay should be exceptional. Our strategic location in Ahmedabad provides easy access to business districts, cultural landmarks, and entertainment hubs. Our 50 beautifully designed rooms combine modern elegance with traditional Indian aesthetics, ensuring comfort and style in every detail.
